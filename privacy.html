Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Onyx Holdings Group ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our messaging and call-handling service (the "Service"), which helps small service-industry businesses communicate with their customers by text message after a missed call.

1. Who We Are

Onyx Holdings Group is a private limited liability company. Our Service is used by independent service-industry businesses (such as plumbers, HVAC technicians, roofers, and electricians) to respond to their own customers' calls when the business owner is unable to answer the phone.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Business account information. When a service-industry business signs up for the Service, we collect the owner's name, email address, phone number, and business details (business name, address, business phone number).
  • End-user caller information. When a customer calls a subscribing business and chooses to continue the conversation by text message (by pressing 2 during the business's voicemail greeting), we collect the caller's phone number and the content of the text messages they send.
  • Conversation content. We store the text messages, voicemail recordings, and voicemail transcriptions that pass through the Service so that the subscribing business can review them.
  • Technical and usage information. Standard device, log, and usage data related to the Service (for example, app version, timestamps, error logs).

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Route calls and text messages between a subscribing business and its own customers.
  • Display conversations, voicemails, and appointments to the subscribing business inside our application.
  • Generate replies on behalf of the subscribing business when the business has enabled the assisted-reply feature.
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use the information to build advertising profiles, and we do not use the content of messages for marketing.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

We share limited information only with the service providers we use to operate the Service, including:

  • Twilio (telecommunications and SMS infrastructure).
  • Google Cloud / Firebase (application backend, database, and authentication).
  • Anthropic (to generate assisted text replies on behalf of the subscribing business).

These providers are bound by contract to use the information only to provide their services to us. We may also disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Onyx Holdings Group, our users, or the public.

5. Mobile Messaging

If you are a customer of a business that uses our Service, text messages are sent to you only after you actively press 2 during the business's voicemail to opt in. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies with the conversation. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and you can request assistance by replying HELP. We do not share mobile opt-in data or consent with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as the subscribing business maintains an account with us, and we retain conversation and voicemail data for so long as it is needed to provide the Service to that business. A subscribing business may delete its account at any time from within the application, at which point associated account data and conversations are deleted from our active systems.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the Service or by other appropriate means.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to Onyx Holdings Group at legal@onyxholdings.group.
